A Message from Our Founder

Growing up, I didn't have the luxury of good clothing or the basic items every child needs to feel prepared for the world. I remember the feeling of staying in my shell, not finding the full expression of my God-given abilities because I lacked the confidence to stand out.

This experience stayed with me. I realized that poverty doesn't just take away resources; it takes away a child's voice. I started this foundation to bridge that gap. We provide the "Alat" (the joy and vibrance) that allows children to break out of their shells and share their talents with the world.
