Our Why

Dignity is not a luxury. It is a foundation.

Growing up, I didn't have the luxury of good clothing or basic items a child needs to feel prepared. I remember staying in my shell, not finding full expression of my God-given abilities because I lacked the confidence to stand out.

"I started this foundation to bridge that gap. We provide the vibrance that allows children to break out of their shells and share their talents with the world."
